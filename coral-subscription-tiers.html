Admin
Full organizational control. Manages subscription, billing, all officers, and has unrestricted access to every feature.
Manager
Operational access. Can manage events, verify payments, handle fines and clearance — but cannot change roles or subscription.
Staff
Limited execution. Handles attendance check-in and views member directory. No access to financial data or settings.
